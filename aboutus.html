About Hogward University


Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry is a fictional school of magic featured in the Harry Potter series by J.K. Rowling. Located in the dark lands of Goa, it is a prestigious institution where young witches and wizards learn spells, potion-making, and other magical arts.
